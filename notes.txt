1. What is Markdown?

- Basically a simplified markup language that is used for formatting text.
- Created in 2004 by John Gruber and Aaron Swartz.
- Widely used in blogging, instant messaging, online forums, collaborative software, documentation pages, and readme files.
- Wiki: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markdown
- Original documentation: https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/

2. Why would you use Markdown?

- Much easier to learn, read and use than HTML.
- There are different flavours of markdown.
